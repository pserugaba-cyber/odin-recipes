Pasta Recipe

Back to the main page
  1. Boil water in a large pot and add a pinch of salt.
  2. Add the pasta to the boiling water and cook according to the package instructions until al dente.
  3. While the pasta is cooking, prepare your sauce. You can use a store-bought sauce or make your own from scratch.
  4. Once the pasta is cooked, drain it in a colander and return it to the pot.
  5. Add the sauce to the pasta and toss to combine.
  6. Serve the pasta hot, garnished with grated Parmesan cheese and fresh herbs if desired.
